# 1st Preliminary Delivery

## Genre
3D Action / Roguelike Shooter

## Core Idea
The player navigates a dangerous 3D environment eliminating enemies and completing objectives, but with a unique twist: death does not reset the game world. Every action permanently alters the environment—enemies stay dead, bodies remain, and progress is retained between reincarnations. The player has a limited number of reincarnations, creating tension and forcing careful, strategic gameplay where every decision matters.

## Mechanics
- **One-Hit Death:** The player dies instantly from a single bullet, emphasizing precision, awareness, and careful movement.

- **Persistent World:** The environment does not reset upon death. Defeated enemies, destroyed objects, and the player’s previous bodies remain in the world permanently.

- **Limited Reincarnations:** The player can only respawn a fixed number of times before reaching a permanent game over.

- **Objective-Based Progression:** Players must complete tasks such as eliminating targets, clearing hostile areas, or surviving enemy encounters.

- **3D Combat & Exploration:** Players move through immersive 3D environments while managing enemy threats, cover, and positioning.

- **Enemy AI:** Enemies patrol areas, detect the player, chase them, and attack when in range, creating tense combat situations.

- **Strategic Gameplay:** Since the player is extremely fragile, success depends on timing, positioning, stealth, and smart decision-making rather than rushing into combat.

## Win / Lose Conditions

### Win Condition
Complete all objectives and survive before running out of reincarnations.

### Lose Condition
Use all available reincarnations, resulting in a permanent game over.
